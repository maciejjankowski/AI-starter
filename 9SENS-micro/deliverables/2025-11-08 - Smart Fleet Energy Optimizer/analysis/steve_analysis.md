## Key Strategic Decisions

### Decision 1: Pricing Model

**Options:**
- A) Flat fee €1,500/mo (like competitors)
- B) Success fee 15% only
- C) Hybrid €1,000 + 15% success fee

**Recommendation**: **B) Success fee 15% only** (pure blue ocean differentiation)

- Eliminates price comparison (can't compare vs flat fee)

- A) 100% direct sales (BDR-driven)
